Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU)

About us:

Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU) is a Public university in New Delhi, India. The university aims to educate students of all ages and backgrounds. The university is a part of the National Open University (NOU) and is affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE).

Our Mission:

Our mission is to provide quality education to the students of the society. We aim to provide academic excellence and to promote the development of the students.

Benefits of Attending Open University:

  • Affordable tuition fees
  • High quality education
  • Vast Educational And Support Network
  • Professional faculty
  • Flexible Timings
